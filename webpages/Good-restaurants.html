Good Restaurants in Jaipur

Jaipur is a food lover’s paradise, offering a diverse range of culinary experiences. From traditional Rajasthani cuisine to international flavors, the city’s dining scene is as vibrant as its culture. Whether you’re a vegetarian or enjoy a mix of cuisines, Jaipur has something for every palate. Here are some of the best restaurants in Jaipur that you must try.

Top Vegetarian Restaurants

Chokhi Dhani

Description: Experience authentic Rajasthani cuisine in a traditional village setting at Chokhi Dhani. Known for its extensive vegetarian thali, this restaurant offers a rich and immersive cultural experience.

Signature Dishes: Dal Baati Churma, Gatte ki Sabzi, Bajra Roti

Location: Tonk Road, Jaipur

Tan Sukh by Kanha

Description: Tan-Sukh by Kanha is a popular vegetarian restaurant offering a delightful range of Indian and Rajasthani dishes. Known for its hygienic environment and delicious food, it’s a favorite spot for families and food enthusiasts.

Signature Dishes:Rajasthani Thali, Paneer Butter Masala, Tandoori Breads

Location:Vaishali Nagar, Jaipur

Laxmi Mishthan Bhandar (LMB)

Description: LMB is a historic eatery in Jaipur, famous for its vegetarian fare. The restaurant serves a variety of traditional sweets and snacks, as well as an elaborate vegetarian thali.

Signature Dishes: Rajasthani Thali, Ghewar, Kachori

Location: Johari Bazaar, Jaipur

Popular Multi-Cuisine Restaurants

The Verandah - Rambagh Palace

Description: The Verandah offers a regal dining experience with a mix of Indian and international cuisines. Set in the luxurious Rambagh Palace, this restaurant is perfect for a special evening out.

Signature Dishes: Grilled Meats, Indian Curries, Decadent Desserts

Location: Rambagh Palace, Jaipur

Tapri Central

Description: A favorite among locals and tourists alike, Tapri Central is known for its quirky ambiance and a menu that blends traditional and modern flavors. It’s a great spot for tea and snacks.

Signature Dishes: Cheese Vada Pav, Tandoori Paneer Sandwich, Masala Tea

Location: C-Scheme, Jaipur

Saffron - Jaipur Marriott

Description: Saffron is an elegant fine-dining restaurant offering North Indian cuisine with a modern twist. The ambiance is sophisticated, making it ideal for a formal dinner.

Signature Dishes: Butter Chicken, Dal Saffron, Garlic Naan

Location: Jaipur Marriott Hotel, Tonk Road, Jaipur

Hidden Gems

Spice Court

Description: Spice Court is a hidden gem known for its traditional Indian dishes and serene courtyard setting. It’s a great place to enjoy a relaxed meal away from the hustle and bustle.

Signature Dishes: Laal Maas, Paneer Tikka, Rajasthani Curries

Location: Civil Lines, Jaipur

Samode Haveli Restaurant

Description: Located within the historic Samode Haveli, this restaurant offers a royal dining experience with a focus on traditional Rajasthani and North Indian cuisines.

Signature Dishes: Thali Platter, Kebabs, Exotic Desserts

Location: Gangapole, Jaipur

Jaipur’s dining scene is as rich and varied as the city itself. Whether you’re indulging in a lavish thali or enjoying a casual meal at a local café, the flavors of Jaipur are sure to leave you craving for more. Don’t miss out on these culinary hotspots during your visit!
